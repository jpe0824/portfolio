About me

Creative developer and life-long learner

I have always loved tech and making things. When I was younger it came in the form of video games like minecraft or some city builder or even Legos. Building in a digital space came naturally. A few years into a sales career and some un-forseen global events, (*cough) I started to play around with computer programming. I was instantly hooked. I started classes right away and haven't really looked back since.

Since then I have been focused on completing my degree and contributing as a developer. My current position has taught me a lot in the front end and I am currently sharpening my skills to work as a full stack dev.

As a developer

I pride myself in putting the best code forward. I take time to ensure my code is readable and maintainable. I am always willing to take feedback and use that to improve my skills.

I am always excited to start a new project. I look to learn something new and am happy to lead the way when needed.

As a colleague

Communication is key. With my background in sales I have learned to communicate with every type of person. I am always happy to voice my opinion or welcome others' as well.

I am a team player and happy to help my peers when needed. I prefer to go into what I call code mode (blast music for a couple hours and vigorously type away) but when needed, jumping on a call to break down a complicated problem can be just as fun.

As a person

Work life balance is very important to me. One of the things that gravitated me to this career was just that. When doing sales I often found myself burned out and performing sub-optimally in my work and life.

I make sure to take time for my family and myself. It puts me in a great place mentally and because of that I am able to produce and out-perform expectations.
